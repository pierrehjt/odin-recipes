Crepes

Description

simple crepes that can easily be made

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • water
  • milk
  • butter

Steps

  1. whisk the eggs, water, and milk together
  2. slowly whisk in the flour and butter
  3. scoop mixture onto griddle or pan
  4. cook until lightly browned
  5. flip and cook until done on other side
  6. remove from cooking surface, finish with sweet or savory toppings
